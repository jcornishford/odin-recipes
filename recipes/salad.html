Cobb Salad

A tasty salad that has vegetables.

Easy to make recipe that is sure to please the family.

Ingredients

  • 6 sticks of bacon
  • 4 boiled eggs
  • 6 cups of chopped lettuce
  • 3 chopped tomatoes
  • 2 cups of blue cheese
  • 1 cup of green onions
  • 2 chopped avocadoes
  • 1 cup of ranch dressing

Steps

  1. Boil,peel,and chop eggs.
  2. Cook,drain,and crumble the bacon.
  3. Assemble the salad.
  4. Drizzle with dressing before serving.
